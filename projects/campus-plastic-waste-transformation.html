Campus Plastic Waste Transformation

Abstract

This project exemplifies the core mission of our Plastic Research Recycling Laboratory, which is dedicated to converting campus-generated plastic waste into valuable products through the application of various plastic recycling methodologies. Our initiative combines environmental sustainability with practical innovation to create a circular economy within our campus community.

Technologies Used

In our state-of-the-art recycling lab, we employ cutting-edge technologies to transform plastic waste into meaningful products:

Figure 1: Plastic Shredder

Breaks down plastic waste into smaller, manageable pieces for processing

Figure 2: Plastic Extruder

Melts and reforms plastic into continuous shapes for new products

Figure 3: Injection Machine

Creates precision plastic components by injecting molten plastic into molds

Products Created

Through innovative recycling processes, we've successfully transformed waste into these functional and beautiful products:

Eco-friendly Benches

Our durable benches made from recycled plastic provide comfortable seating across campus while demonstrating the potential of recycled materials. Each bench represents approximately 200kg of diverted plastic waste.

Wall Clock

This stylish timepiece combines functionality with sustainability, featuring a casing made entirely from recycled plastics. The clock mechanism is designed for longevity, with replaceable components.

Decorative Items and Accessories

From colorful wall art to practical accessories, we've created a range of items that showcase the versatility of recycled plastics. These products help raise awareness about recycling possibilities.

Impact and Future Work

Our initiative has made significant strides in sustainable waste management and community engagement:

Waste Reduction

Diverted over 2 tons of plastic from landfills through our recycling programs

Innovation

Developed 12+ product designs from recycled materials

Community Engagement

Involved 200+ students in recycling workshops and initiatives

Looking ahead, we aim to expand our capabilities with advanced sorting technologies, develop partnerships with local businesses, and create an educational program to teach sustainable manufacturing practices.
