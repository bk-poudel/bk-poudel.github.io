Campus Plastic Waste Transformation

Abstract

This project exemplifies the core mission of our Plastic Research Recycling Laboratory, which is dedicated to converting campus-generated plastic waste into valuable products through the application of various plastic recycling methodologies.

Technologies Used

In our lab, we utilize various technologies to convert plastic waste into meaningful products. These include:

  • Plastic Shredder: Breaks down plastic waste into smaller pieces.
  • Injection Machine: Melts and reforms plastic into different mold shapes.
  • Extruder: Melts and reforms plastic into new shapes.
  • Power Tools: Used for cutting, shaping, and finishing recycled plastic products.

Impact and Future Work

Our lab work has had a significant impact on reducing plastic waste on campus. Key achievements include:

  • Reduction in plastic waste sent to landfills
  • Creation of useful products from recycled plastic
  • Increased awareness and participation in recycling initiatives

We aim to expand our efforts and explore new technologies to further enhance our recycling capabilities.
